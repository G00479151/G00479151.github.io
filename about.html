About ColourBrush

At ColourBrush, We believe self-expression should be effortless and fun!
Our goal was to create a smarter, easier way to experiment with colour
anytime you want. we beileve it's important, especially for young people
to express themselves freely, without worrying about paying €200+
at the hairdressers or the worry of damaging your young healthy hair.

By combining innovative design with smart technology, we created
a revolutionary beauty tool that lets you transform your hair instantly
with just a brush.

Completely damage-free! No Bleach, No Ammonia, No PPD.

How to use:

The brush is rechargable and comes with a USB-C lead. Afer charging your brush, power it on and press the bluetooth on the handle.

Next, download our app, Colour Brush, there you can find a selection of over 200 shades, adjust vibrancy levels.

Gently brush through wet or damp hair, for best results split hair into small sections and brush through thoroughly.

Allow your hair to dry and see the vibrant colour! Typically lasts up
to 5 days before you ned to reapply
